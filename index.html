About Me

I am a front-end developer who builds clean, intuitive, and user-centric web experiences. My approach is uniquely shaped by a 15-year career in healthcare as a Doctor of Pharmacy and strategic writer. The same precision, empathy, and problem-solving skills I used to manage patient care and lead a successful business are now applied to writing clean code and designing effective digital solutions. This background has given me a deep appreciation for the end-user, allowing me to translate complex requirements into accessible and engaging interfaces.

My passion so far has been partnering with small businesses and mission-driven organizations to help them thrive online. I specialize in turning a client's vision into a responsive, accessible website backed by a thoughtful content strategy. For example, I helped a local business achieve a 7x increase in search visibility after launching a new site I developed. I am dedicated to using my technical skills honed through self-study with resources like The Odin Project and DevSlopes to build not just websites, but tools that help organizations connect with their communities.



My skills include:

  • HTML5 & CSS3
  • JavaScript
  • Git & GitHub
  • Responsive Design
